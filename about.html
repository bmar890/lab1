About Me

My name is Karyn and I'm a nature, portrait and artistic photographer based in Provo, Utah. I'm currently a student at BYU and I'm happily engaged to my wonderful fiance Brennan who makes me laugh every single day. I started photography back in 2017 and since then I've been permanently stuck seeing the world through a camera's lens. I love finding tiny details, moments and beauties in the world that most people don't notice. My aim is to make you feel good in a photo and to just have fun! Photography to me is documenting moments and creating art that you can enjoy for years to come. If you want to work with me shoot me a message and we'll get started on making beautiful memories!
